## Task Description (4 variants)

1. Your objective is to optimize the bidding strategy to achieve the target cost per acquisition of {cpa}, while balancing conversion volume and cost efficiency throughout the campaign.
2. The goal is to maintain cost per acquisition at the target level of {cpa}, ensuring that both conversion volume and cost efficiency are appropriately balanced based on current market conditions.
3. Optimize bidding decisions to meet the CPA target of {cpa}, taking into account the trade-off between maximizing conversions and maintaining cost efficiency.
4. Your task is to determine optimal bid levels that will achieve the target CPA of {cpa}, while considering both the opportunity to capture conversions and the need to control costs.
